Smart Living in Indramayu

Voice Note: A brief explanation of the implementation of Smart Living concepts in Indramayu Regency, covering health, education, security, and the environment by Alfath Adia Yahya Kuswandi.

Smart Living in Indramayu focuses on improving the quality of life through innovation in health, education, security, and a sustainable environment. The use of digital technology and cross-sector collaboration are key to creating a comfortable, safe, and inclusive city life.

The Indramayu Regency Government has launched the Indramayu Safe initiative with the installation of more than 400 active CCTV cameras at strategic points. All cameras are connected to the SmartCity Command Center to monitor the area in real-time and speed up the handling of public reports.
