Smart City Transformation in Indramayu

Indramayu embraces digital transformation to build a smarter, greener, and more connected future. Through innovation, technology, and public participation, SmartCity Indramayu improves mobility, governance, economy, environment, and quality of life.

Six Pillars of SmartCity Indramayu

Smart Governance

Enhancing transparency, public access, and government efficiency using digital platforms.

Smart People

Improving digital literacy, creativity, and participation to create an inclusive society.

Smart Mobility

Developing efficient, safe, and environmentally friendly transportation systems.

Smart Environment

Implementing sustainability, renewable energy systems, and eco-friendly solutions.

Smart Economy

Increasing productivity, innovation, and competitiveness driven by technology.

Smart Living

Improving public service quality such as health, safety, and education.

About SmartCity Indramayu

SmartCity Indramayu is an innovation-driven regional development program initiated by the Government of Indramayu Regency. It aligns with the national Smart City blueprint and focuses on modern public services, data-driven planning, ICT infrastructure, environmental sustainability, and community involvement. The six pillars help transform Indramayu into a modern, future-ready regional model.
