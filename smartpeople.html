Smart People in Indramayu

Voice Note: A brief explanation of smart people initiatives and community development in Indramayu Regency by Darmawan Almadani.

Human Capital & Education

One of the main focuses of Smart People in Indramayu is enhancing human resources through education and digital training. The local government works together with educational institutions and local communities to improve digital literacy and encourage the community to adapt to technology.

  • Indramayu Smart Digital program, which trains students and SMEs to use digital apps and social media productively.
  • Development of Smart Classrooms in several schools with internet-based devices and online learning.
  • Collaboration with universities to support research and community-based technological innovation.

Digital Literacy & Innovation

The level of digital literacy in the community has continued to improve thanks to various training programs and awareness campaigns. According to local surveys, the number of people capable of operating digital apps has significantly increased in the past two years.

Community Engagement & Social Inclusion

The Smart People program also emphasizes the importance of public participation, information transparency, and collaboration among citizens. Through online platforms like community forums and official social media, citizens can contribute ideas and report issues in their environments.

  • Online discussion forums for community aspirations and innovation.
  • Workforce skills training programs for women and rural youth.
  • Smart Citizen initiative to strengthen digital ethics and social responsibility in cyberspace.
